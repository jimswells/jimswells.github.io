Welcome to The Yellow Band Project

Whenever Owen was asked that age old question of what do you want to do when you grow up, he always had the same answer. He wanted to make people happy. You could see it in the way he treated others, even complete strangers. Asking how a persons day was going. Holding doors open for people. Greeting people by name. His goal was always the same, to make people smile. To share his joy with others wherever he went.

On November 17th, 2022 Owen was killed in a hit and run accident. In his memory, his family and friends have taken his passion for spreading kindness and expanded it throughout their communities. The yellow bands are a reminder of living that mission out daily, to radiate joy and spread kindness everywhere.


If you would like to be an ambassador in your community and spread the mission of joy and kindness we would love to send you some yellow bands for you to give out whenever you see kindness and joy being displayed. Become an Ambassador.
